Case Studies

Sentinel Investigation Platform

Graph-based threat intelligence & decision support

A platform that maps relationships across phishing domains, campaigns, organizations, and threat actors to support faster, evidence-driven investigations.

  • Problem: Fraud and phishing signals are scattered across sources, making attribution slow and manual.
  • Approach: Modeled entities and relationships in a Neo4j graph and layered risk scoring, clustering, and timeline analysis on top.
  • Outcome: Guided investigation workflows that turn fragmented intelligence into prioritized, actionable decisions.
View Project →

Rotaj EV Optimization

Optimization & decision support for mobility systems

A decision-support platform that recommends efficient EV charging using multi-source operational and environmental data.

  • Problem: EV charging decisions depend on shifting demand, station availability, energy, and weather conditions.
  • Approach: Integrated energy, weather, charging-station, and mobility datasets into optimization and decision-support logic.
  • Outcome: Recommendations that balance availability and efficiency for drivers and infrastructure planning.
View Project →

ENKA AI NCR Root Cause Analysis

Quality engineering & decision-support concept

A concept exploring AI-assisted decision support for Non-Conformance Report (NCR) analysis, informed by quality-management work during my ENKA internship.

  • Problem: NCR analysis often relies on manual documentation review, slowing root cause identification.
  • Approach: Modeled decision-support logic using quality-management frameworks and root cause analysis methods.
  • Outcome: Concept prototype illustrating faster, more consistent AI-assisted quality diagnostics.
